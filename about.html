About us

With extensive experience in cleantech, lithium processing systems, and industrial equipment, we founded Upanishad Solutions to bridge the gap between complex technical solutions and practical implementation.

Our experience working with lithium extraction systems and pumping rig technologies has taught us that the best tech solutions are the ones that teams can actually build, operate, and maintain. That's the philosophy behind every project we take on.

When we're not designing mechanical systems, you'll find us working on DIY automation projects or helping local businesses streamline their operations with better documentation and processes.
